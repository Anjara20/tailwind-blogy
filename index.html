Ten tips for productive meetings

Most people don’t like to go to meetings; they disrupt work flow and are generally inconvenient. When planning a meeting, ask yourself if a face-to-face gathering of people is really necessary. Can you accomplish your goal on a conference call? A meeting that has binders or other handouts or is scheduled to last more than an hour should be done face-to-face.
