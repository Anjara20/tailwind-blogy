Business

Ten tips for productive meetings

Most people don’t like to go to meetings; they disrupt work flow and are generally inconvenient. When planning a meeting, ask yourself if a face-to-face gathering of people is really necessary. Can you accomplish your goal on a conference call? A meeting that has binders or other handouts or is scheduled to last more than an hour should be done face-to-face. Meeting to delegate known tasks between peers or to discuss followup actions required in an ongoing project can and should be done electronically. Everyone will thank you for respecting their time whether they say so or not.
